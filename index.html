About Pennsylvania

Pennsylvania is a Middle Atlantic state on the Eastern Seaboard of the United States. It is known for its important role in American history, its varied geography, and its mix of major metropolitan areas and rural regions. The state capital is Harrisburg, while Philadelphia and Pittsburgh are its two largest and most well-known cities. [P1]

Quick Facts

  1. Pennsylvania’s capital city is Harrisburg, which serves as the center of state government. [P1]
  2. Pennsylvania is known as the Keystone State because of its important role in early American history. [P2]
  3. Pennsylvania became a state in 1787 and was one of the original thirteen states. [P1]
  4. Pennsylvania is located in the northeastern United States and includes both large cities and rural areas. [P1]
